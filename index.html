Headliners

Nat Seelen

Nat Seelen is a clarinetist, composer, and founder of the Boston Festival of New Jewish Music, known for his work with the internationally acclaimed Ezekiel’s Wheels Klezmer Band. With deep roots in klezmer, jazz, and classical music, Nat brings a sharp, soulful sound and bold creativity to every stage he plays.

Neta Weiner

Neta Weiner is a boundary-pushing Israeli artist known for fusing spoken word, hip-hop, and traditional Jewish sounds into a vibrant, genre-defying performance. As the frontman of the acclaimed band System Ali, Neta’s multilingual lyrics weave together Hebrew, Arabic, Russian, and Yiddish, creating a powerful tapestry of identity, resistance, and community. His music is both deeply personal and fiercely political — a call to connection through rhythm and truth.

Beth Bahia Cohen

Beth Bahia Cohen is a master violinist of Syrian and Russian Jewish heritage whose music bridges cultures from Greece to the Middle East. With a global palette of bowed instruments and a career spanning orchestras, world ensembles, and film scores, Beth brings extraordinary depth, versatility, and soul to every performance.
